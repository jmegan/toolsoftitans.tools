FAQ

General

Are you affliated with Tim Ferriss or the publisher?
No. We are not (yet) connected with Tim or the publisher. Just a really big fan of his and this book!

What exactly is included in this guide?
Throughout Tools of Titans, within the chapters about each individual, aka 'Titan', many brand names, products, books, tools, gadgets, food, etc. are mentioned. This is a collection of almost every specific 'Tool' stated throughout the book. There are many times where Tim mentions to Google a name or phase. Well in this guide we have already done that for you.

What 'Tools' are not included?
  • Many of the Titan's are authors and within their introductions it states (mostly all) of their written books. If the author has written 1-2 books, they are mentioned. If the author has more than 2+ books, they are not included (but can be) unless they were specifically mentioned within the chapters. For example, Seth Godin has written 20+ books and adding all of his books will be confusing along with the other tools he recommended.
  • Throughout the 'Healthy' section, many exercises are mentioned. These specific exercises and picture are not included but can be upon request.

How did you choose this specific products?
This site is not affliated with any sponsors. If the specific brand name of a tool was mentioned that item is included. Any generic items and #1 top result / best-selling item was included.

You're using affiliate links to Amazon?
Yes. Indexing and compiling all of this content took a considerable amount of time, along with the purchase of javascript plugins, the user interface, and server to keep this site up and running at top speeds; a small donation will help a young developer greatly.

There are tools that state they are recommended by Tim Ferriss but are within a chapter about a Titan. How come?
Throughout the book, Tim gives his own thoughts (labled by 'TF:') on what the Titan said. Within these sections Tim often recommends specific tools he uses related to what was said. Even though the specific Titan did not mention / recommend that tool, since Tim is stating this tool is relatable to with the Titan states, we included that the tool is recommended by the Titan.

Is every Titan here in this guide?
Not every Titan / chapter mentions a specific tool they use or recommend. They are still listed on the Titan page though.

Is there an index for this book?
YES! Tim Ferriss recently announced the index for this book in January. You can download it from here.
